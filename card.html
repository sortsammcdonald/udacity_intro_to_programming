Cairn Terrier

It is quite common for Cairn Terriers' fur colour to change during their lifetimes. A Cairn Terrier may be born with blonde fur that becomes much darker as it ages. Also Donatha's dog in the Wizard of Oz was a Carian Terrier

  • Origin: Scotland
  • Height: 22-33cm
  • Weight: 4.5-7.3kg
  • Life span: 12-17 years

Amongst the oldest terrier breeds, KT's are orginally from the Scottish Highlands. Originally breed as a working dog to hunt rats, they are active dogs that require daily walking but are also obient and easily trained.
